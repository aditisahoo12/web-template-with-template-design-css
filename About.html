About Me

Hello! I'm Aditi, a passionate and versatile web developer with a deep love for crafting intuitive and user-friendly websites. My journey in web development began with a fascination for how code can bring ideas to life, and over the years, it has evolved into a career where I constantly challenge myself to learn new technologies, tools, and techniques.

I specialize in building responsive, high-performance websites that prioritize the user experience. My skills range from front-end design using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to creating dynamic web applications. Additionally, I have a strong background in social media marketing, which helps me ensure that websites not only perform well but also reach the right audience.

My goal is to create digital experiences that are both visually stunning and highly functional, whether it's for a small business looking to establish an online presence or a large company needing a complex web solution. I'm always excited to work on projects that push the boundaries of web development and bring fresh ideas to the table.
